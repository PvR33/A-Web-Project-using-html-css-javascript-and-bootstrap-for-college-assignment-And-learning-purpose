Paras Education Trust

S D Jain Modern School (Managed by Paras Education Trust) was set up in 1999 in the fond memory of Late Smt. Sharbati Devi Jain (SDJ), mother of founder chairman Late Shri Devraj Jain. That was the tribute by the Jain Family to their beloved mother. The major objective of starting an English Medium CBSE Co Education School was to provide world-class education in Surat and to provide quality learning to the all the sections of society. School is propelled by a team of competent, dedicated teachers and staff, supported by infrastructure par excellence. Presently School has more than 4500 Students. It stands amongst the best rated CSBE schools in Surat. In furtherance to the quest of Quality Higher Education, Chairman of Paras Group of Industries, Shri Kailashchand Jain, started SDJ International College in 2012, with an aim to become the best under-graduation English medium college in Surat, providing career oriented real value addition & practical exposure to the Students. Along with SDJ International College, SDJ Group has set up SDJ Professional Academy to cater quality professional & vocational courses, which can generate ample employment opportunities, like Certification in Export Import Management, Photography, Event Management, Fashion Designing, School of Music, Chartered Financial Analyst, Entrepreneurship and many more.
