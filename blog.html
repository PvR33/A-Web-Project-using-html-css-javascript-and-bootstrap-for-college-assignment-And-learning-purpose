Introduction

Jain, currently headed by Shri Kailashchand Jain (Group Chairman) and managed by the Jain Family. Shri Kailashchand Jain has been the backbone of the Group with wide and extensive experience in various segments. The Group has diversified interests in various sectors like Textiles, Exports, Education, Ship Breaking, Chemicals, Aluminum Foil, Construction, Real Estate, and Taking over of Assets of the Sick Companies. Its working horizons have spread over Surat, Sachin, Ankleshwar, Valia, Bhavnagar, Palsana and Jammu. S D Jain Modern School (Managed by Paras Education Trust) was set up in 1999 in the fond memory of Late Smt. Sharbati Devi Jain (SDJ), mother of founder chairman Late Shri Devraj Jain. That was the tribute by the Jain Family to their beloved mother. The major objective of starting an English Medium CBSE Co Education School was to provide world-class education in Surat and to provide quality learning to the all the sections of society. School is propelled by a team of competent, dedicated teachers and staff, supported by infrastructure par excellence. Presently School has more than 4500 Students. It stands amongst the best rated CSBE schools in Surat. In furtherance to the quest of Quality Higher Education, Chairman of Paras Group of Industries, Shri Kailashchand Jain, started SDJ International College in 2012, with an aim to become the best under-graduation English medium college in Surat, providing career oriented real value addition & practical exposure to the Students. Along with SDJ International College, SDJ Group has set up SDJ Professional Academy to cater quality professional & vocational courses, which can generate ample employment opportunities, like Certification in Export Import Management, Photography, Event Management, Fashion Designing, School of Music, Chartered Financial Analyst, Entrepreneurship and many more.

Facilities

  • We take pride in offering state-of-the-art facilities including modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, a library, and more.
  • Library, the resource center of the institution is a storehouse of 2100+ books of renowned authors and publications to enrich the knowledge of our students. The subscription of national journals, periodicals and newspapers keep the students updated with the events happening across the globe. Using the E-library, the students browse the internet and delve into various topics that add value to their research.
  • The institution is a Wi-Fi campus having internet broadband connectivity with an 11MBPS point-to-point internet leased line. The two Wi-Fi routers ensure good connectivity in the lab, staffroom, and admin area and are also available to students for research purposes.
  • The college has a departmental-based laboratories facility for the students.
  • The college has an auditorium facility for events and seminars.
  • The college has a canteen facility for staff and students.
  • The college has sports facilities for all students.
  • SDJIC has two technologically sound computer labs with 70 computers in each. This ensures a dedicated two hours lab time for each student on daily basis. The role of a laboratory is to educate undergraduate students in the concepts and techniques of modern intelligence. The computer lab remains open 8 hours a day, 6 days a week, and is staffed with helpful consultants. The lab is supported by a 15KVA online UPS, which is able to take over the operations of a computer in case of power failure. The UPS is currently having power back up to 40 minutes.
